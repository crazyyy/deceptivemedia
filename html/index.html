Welcome

Posted: Andy Bell on 15 November 2010

Hi I’m Andy Bell (aka Deceptive). I’m a mainly abstract photographer based in Godalming, Surrey, United Kingdom. Concerned mainly with colour, lines and shapes. Deceptive Media is my award winning photoblog and portfolio and has been on line for 13 years, 8 months, and 20 days . For print/download sales or general enquires contact me by sending me an email . You can also find me at Twitter, Instagram and Facebook.
